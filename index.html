Privacy Policy – Moj Prozor Svjetla

Moj Prozor Svjetla is a faith-based inspirational Android application.

Data Collection

  • The app does NOT collect, transmit, or store personal data for the developer.
  • The app does NOT require account creation.
  • The app does NOT share data with third parties.

Local Data

  • User-created notes, favorite quotes, and quote history are stored locally on the user's device.

Backup

  • If the user explicitly and optionally chooses to sign in with a Google account, the app can store a backup of local app data in the user's own Google Drive AppData folder.
  • This backup is private, encrypted, and inaccessible to the developer.

Permissions

  • Internet: used only when the user initiates Google Drive backup or restore.
  • Notifications: used for daily inspirational notifications.
  • Microphone (optional): used only for speech-to-text features when explicitly enabled by the user.

Data Deletion

  • Users can delete locally stored data at any time from within the app using the “Clear” button for quote history and favorite quotes.
  • All remaining local data can also be removed by uninstalling the app.
